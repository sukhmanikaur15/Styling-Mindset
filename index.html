"FASHION TRENDS" come and go; meanwhile a society's values are established and evolving characteristics to their beliefs and culture. Fashion is just not an ambitious projected image of a reinterpreted good old value to fulfill some funtion or agenda alike but rather a evocative and refreshing concept worthy enough to be portrayed for society's appreciation that makes us even more instinctive.

The success of the fashion trend lies in the way the society interprets the fashion trend and judges it.

In fact clothing and fashion are carriers of wide range of idelogical meanings. The fashion trends portays a visual culture and style of dressing. The social agenda of these fashion trends is to express the consumer's identity in terms of lifestyle choices and their attitude.

When a particular style of dress comes in a vogue or used by film stars and models, it is blindly followed by college students.
