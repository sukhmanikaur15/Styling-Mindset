Call it changing mindset or an effect of globalization, the western wear is spreading across India rapidly. Modern women today feel more comfortable wearing western wear than most traditional clothes.

When people wear clothes that fit, they look much better than when they wear loose or tight clothes.

In the case of Salwars’, most women tend to wear loose, salwars and even the tops tend to be loose and flowy and the dupatta also tends to add more layers of clothes. All this extra cloth makes a person seem a little older. Plus, most women don’t tend to pick exciting colours — most salwars are often in bland, dull colours.

In the case of jeans, most jeans tend to hug the shape of the body and have very little extra/loose cloth. The shirts also tend to fit well. Plus, there are a ton of options in terms of colour, style, logos, etc.
